Welcome to 6.S198, Deep Learning Practicum

This course encourages students to carry out projects that use deep learning. Students use state-of-the-art techniques such as convolutional neural networks, recurrent neural networks, generative adversarial networks, and embedding to solve modern problems. This site is meant to showcase the final project presented by the students at the end of the semester.
